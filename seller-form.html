Seller form → score & sort sellers

Turning the seller form into a way to find your best sellers

Mohamed Abuissa
GTM Engineer · RevOps
Prepared for Trofee
Your current form collects sellers. This version makes it score and sort them, so your best applicants get a personal message within 24 hours and the rest self-serve. Two changes do the heavy lifting: ask for the things that actually predict how much a seller will sell (audience size + whether they've sold live), and fill in the rest automatically so the form stays short.

4 Auto-fill (Clay)

Runs automatically when they apply, so the form stays short.
  • Pull follower count + engagement from IG / TikTok / YouTube handle
  • Check if they already sell live (Whatnot / eBay Live)
  • Work out category + how often they post from recent posts
  • Remove duplicates against your CRM
  • Flag fakes / restricted goods
apply → auto-fill → score → save tier to CRM → send to the right person: Tier A triggers a WhatsApp + task within 24h

5 Auto-decline

Overrides the score. A high score never beats a deal-breaker.
Legal / trust
  • Counterfeits, replicas, or "rep" goods
  • Restricted / regulated items; under-18; out-of-region
Behaviour
  • Won't go live (the whole point)
  • Wants list-only, eBay-style, no shows
Money
  • Baskets too small to cover payment, shipping & support
